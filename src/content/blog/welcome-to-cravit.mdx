## What Is Cravit?

Cravit is a social food-discovery app built around one core idea: **personal food missions called Cravies**.

<ImageFull src="/mockups/Home.png" alt="Cravit home screen showing active Cravies" caption="Your Cravies dashboard â€” all your food missions in one place" />

A Cravy is simple. You create a mission â€” like "Find the best smash burger in Milan" or "Our group's new favorite aperitivo bar" â€” set the criteria that matter to you (taste, vibe, price, portion size), add spots you want to try, invite your friends, and log reviews as you visit each place.

At the end, you get a leaderboard showing which spot actually wins. No spreadsheets. No scattered notes. No confusion about where you're actually going next.

## Why We Built This

The story's simple. When I moved to Milan, I found myself in the same situation three times with different groups of friends:

1. Trying to find "our pub" â€” that one place that becomes *your* spot (like McLaren's from How I Met Your Mother)
2. On a mission to find the best smash burger in the city
3. Hunting for the perfect Sunday breakfast spot with my roommate

Every time, we ended up creating random notes, spreadsheets, Instagram saves. Nothing organized. Nothing fun. And we never actually knew which place won.

After doing this three times in three completely different ways, I thought: *why doesn't this exist as an app?*

So we built it.
